My name is Janessa. I hope to make the internet easy to use and accessible for all types of people as a web developer.

About Me

An alumnus of Brigham Young University, Janessa received two Bachelor of Arts degrees in Linguistics and Italian Studies in 2018. As an undergraduate student, she taught a university-level Italian grammar class, was an ORCA grant recipient, and received a Superior rating on the Italian OPI. Upon graduating, Janessa was a localization intern for Cake Technologies, Inc. Currently, she is a full stack web development student at Lambda School.

Outside of her studies and work endeavors, Janessa served a church mission in Italy for 18 months from 2013-2014. She learned firsthand that the best pizza is in Naples and the best lasagna has bechamel sauce. Janessa also enjoys running, cooking, watching movies, and spending time with her family. Her husband, Peter, is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Information Systems Management at Brigham Young University. Their daughter, Kora, recently turned one! As such, her mom hasn't had a ton of time to pursue her extracurricular activities, but baby smiles and snuggles make things better.
